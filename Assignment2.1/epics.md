# Epics — Conference Room Booking System



## Epic 1: Core Booking Engine

> Covers the fundamental capabilities that allow employees to find, reserve, and manage room bookings. This epic is the foundation of the system — all other epics depend on it being stable.

- Story #1: Basic Room Booking
- Story #2: Recurring Meeting Setup
- Story #4: Booking Cancellation

---

## Epic 2: Room Discovery & Filtering

> Covers the features that help employees narrow down room options by their meeting's specific needs, including headcount and required equipment.

- Story #3: Room Capacity Filtering
- Story #5: Room Equipment Requirements

---

## Epic 3: Administration & Oversight

> Covers the tools available to the Admin role for monitoring all bookings, resolving conflicts, and generating insights from usage data.

- Story #6: Admin Dashboard Viewing
- Story #9: Booking Conflict Resolution
- Story #10: Usage Reports Generation

---

## Epic 4: Facilities & Maintenance

> Covers the tools used by the Facilities Manager to keep room data accurate and to block rooms from bookings during maintenance periods.

- Story #7: Room Maintenance Scheduling

---

## Epic 5: Reception & Visitor Management

> Covers the tools used by the Receptionist to assist walk-in visitors and external guests who require meeting space without system credentials.

- Story #8: Visitor Booking Assistance
