# Sprint 1 Review

Date: 2026-05-13
Sprint Goal: Enable employees to successfully book and manage conference rooms without conflicts, while giving admins real-time visibility and control over all bookings.

## Sprint Goal Outcome

Partially Met.

Four out of five stories were completed and passed all their acceptance criteria. Employees can now search for rooms, book them, filter by capacity, and cancel bookings. The Admin Dashboard is also live and showing real time room status. Story 9 about Booking Conflict Resolution was not fully finished. The conflict detection part works but the resolution screen and the audit trail logging still need to be completed.

## Stakeholder Feedback

Positive feedback: The Product Owner said the booking flow is smooth and easy to use. She was happy that the capacity filter was ready because she did not expect it this sprint. The Admin Dashboard polling feature was well received by the facilities team.

Concerns or questions: The Product Owner asked about the email confirmation still running on a mock service and wants to know when the real integration will be done before the system goes live. Someone from the Facilities team asked if Story 7 about Maintenance Scheduling could be moved earlier because of upcoming office renovations.

Requests or adjustments: The Product Owner requested that Story 9 must be the very first thing finished in Sprint 2 before any new work begins. The request to move Story 7 earlier will be discussed during Sprint 2 planning.
